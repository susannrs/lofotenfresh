Lofoten Fresh

Lofoten Fresh is a newly created food truck that aims to give the locals and tourists of Lofoten a fresh treat. The food truck slogan is "fast slow-food". With that, the food truck wants to give the customers another option relative to the traditional restaurants, as well as deliver the ascetically and tastefully dishes in record time.

Sigur Skarby

Sigur Skarby has a long experience with cooking and is a trained chef. He got the sudden realization that he needed to give the locals and tourists of Lofoten a taste of its fresh ingredients. The result was Lofoten Fresh. Sigur is born and raised in Lofoten, which makes him the perfect mediator of what Lofoten has to give.
